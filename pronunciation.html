Pronunciation & Alphabet

The key to being able to understand any language properly is to have correct pronunciation. Thankfully, Ojibwe has a pretty easy system to learn for English speakers. Spelling will also be covered in this section because the vast majority of the time Ojibwe is spoken exactly as it is written. I will be using the Latin alphabet for the entire site, but will include the syllabary used in Canada here as a reference. In order to hear each sound, click on the corresponding IPA symbol located in the right column. To listen to the IPA sounds, go to ipachart.com.
